Do you have an inquiry that's not addressed here? Please contact us or call us at 011 665 9865.

Are you a registered and compliant company?

Yes. We are fully registered (2019/045633/07), CIDB graded (10230376), CSD registered (MAAA0966253), and fully tax compliant. We strictly adhere to the Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) Act on all sites.

What areas do you service?

Our head office is in Krugersdorp, Gauteng. We primarily service the entire Gauteng province, but we are equipped and willing to travel for large-scale projects across South Africa.

Do you only work on large commercial projects?

While we specialize in major infrastructure projects for SOEs and network operators, we also handle private, residential, and smaller-scale commercial work. No project is too small for our dedication to quality.

What is your B-BBEE status?

We are extremely proud to be a Level 1 B-BBEE Contributor. We are also a 100% Youth- and Female-Owned Enterprise, making us an ideal partner for corporate procurement scorecards.

Do you offer plant hire without operators?

We generally supply equipment with highly skilled, certified operators to ensure safety, efficiency, and to prevent equipment damage. However, terms can be discussed depending on your specific contractual needs.
