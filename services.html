Division 01

Construction & Civil Works

We build foundations you can trust. Our civil works division handles complex infrastructure projects with precision engineering and a strict adherence to safety standards. From ground-breaking to final finishing, we deliver durability.

  • Trenching & Excavation: Precision earthworks for all infrastructure needs.
  • Manhole Builds: Custom construction and installation of telecommunication and utility manholes.
  • Formwork Erection: Structural support and shaping for concrete applications.
  • Concrete & Steel Works: Reinforcing, pouring, and finishing structural concrete.
Division 02

Telecommunications Infrastructure

Keeping South Africa connected. We are a trusted partner to the nation's leading network operators, delivering fast, reliable, and technically flawless fibre installations across urban and remote environments.

  • Fibre Floating & Hauling: High-speed cable deployment through complex duct networks.
  • Cable Jointing & Splicing: Precision optical alignment for zero-loss connectivity.
  • Termination & Testing: OTDR testing and final network commissioning.
  • FTTH / FTTB Installations: End-to-end rollout for Fibre-to-the-Home and Business.
Division 03

Vegetation Management

Protecting critical infrastructure from the elements. We provide safe, environmentally compliant vegetation control for power lines, railway corridors, and industrial sites, preventing service disruptions and mitigating fire risks.

  • Tree Pruning & Felling: Safe clearance of high-risk trees near power lines and structures.
  • Grass Cutting & Slashing: Maintaining servitudes, substations, and highway verges.
  • Herbicide Application: Controlled chemical treatments for long-term weed management.
  • Site Clearing: Preparation for new construction and infrastructure development.
