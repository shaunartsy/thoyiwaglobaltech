### GENERAL & COMPANY

---

**Q: What is Thoyiwa Global Technology?**

**A:** Thoyiwa Global Technology (Pty) Ltd is a 100% youth- and female-owned multi-disciplinary service provider based in Krugersdorp, Gauteng. Established in 2019, we specialise in three core service areas: **Construction & Civil Works**, **Telecommunications Infrastructure**, and **Vegetation Management**. We serve clients across multiple provinces in South Africa, including major operators like MTN, Vodacom, Telkom, Eskom, and Gautrain-affiliated contractors.

---

**Q: What does "Thoyiwa" mean?**

**A:** "Thoyiwa" means *"carry forward"*. It reflects our mission to carry forward the ambitions of our clients, the aspirations of our team, and the economic empowerment of South Africa's youth and women in the infrastructure sector.

---

**Q: Is Thoyiwa Global Technology a B-BBEE compliant company?**

**A:** Yes. Thoyiwa Global Technology is a **100% youth- and female-owned** company, qualifying as a **Level 1 B-BBEE contributor**. Partnering with us supports your organisation's B-BBEE procurement scorecard and transformation objectives.

---

**Q: Where is Thoyiwa Global Technology based?**

**A:** Our head office is located at **32 Morcom Street, Krugersdorp, Gauteng, South Africa**. While we are based in Krugersdorp, we serve clients across Gauteng, Mpumalanga, Limpopo, North West, and other provinces as required by project scope.

---

**Q: How long has Thoyiwa been in business?**

**A:** We were established in **2019** and have been delivering quality infrastructure services for over 6 years. During this time, we've built a strong portfolio of completed projects and trusted client relationships across all three of our service divisions.

---

**Q: What provinces do you operate in?**

**A:** We primarily operate in **Gauteng** but have successfully delivered projects in **Mpumalanga** (Nelspruit), **Limpopo** (Burgersfort), **North West** (Potchefstroom), and the **Tshwane Metropolitan** area. We're willing to mobilise to any location in South Africa based on project requirements.

### TELECOMMUNICATIONS

---

**Q: What telecommunications services do you provide?**

**A:** Our Telecommunications Infrastructure division provides:
- **Fibre floating** — installation of fibre optic cables through duct systems
- **Cable jointing** — connecting cable segments for seamless connectivity
- **Fibre splicing** — fusion splicing for low-loss fibre connections
- **Telephone cable installation**
- **Coaxial (coax) cable installation**
- **Twin flex cable installation**
- **Trunking and PVC conduit installation**

We deliver end-to-end telecommunications infrastructure for both underground and aerial networks.

---

**Q: Which network operators have you worked with?**

**A:** We have delivered projects for South Africa's leading network operators and their appointed contractors, including:
- **MTN** (via Ruantel Telecoms — Huawei partnership)
- **Vodacom** (via Matlight Agency — FTTH rollout)
- **Telkom / Openserve / Nokia** (via Motata and ZealPoint — FTTH aerial installations)
- **Vumatel** (via TSI — FTTB projects)
- **Dark Fibre Africa (DFA)** (via MCT — FTTH deployment)

---

**Q: What is FTTH and FTTB?**

**A:**
- **FTTH (Fibre to the Home):** The installation of fibre optic cables directly to residential homes, providing high-speed internet access.
- **FTTB (Fibre to the Business):** Similar to FTTH, but designed for commercial and business premises, often with higher-specification infrastructure to support business-grade services.

We deliver both FTTH and FTTB installations to the standards set by South Africa's network operators.

---

**Q: Do you do fibre maintenance and fault repairs?**

**A:** Yes. In addition to new installations, we provide ongoing fibre network maintenance, fault location, cable repair, and splice repairs. Our teams use OTDR (Optical Time Domain Reflectometer) testing to identify and resolve faults quickly and accurately.

---

**Q: Can you install both underground and aerial fibre?**

**A:** Yes. We are experienced in both **underground** (ducted/direct-buried) and **aerial** (pole-mounted) fibre installations. Our Telkom FTTH aerial projects in Germiston and Laudium are examples of our aerial deployment capabilities.

### VEGETATION MANAGEMENT

---

**Q: What vegetation management services do you offer?**

**A:** Our Vegetation Management division provides:
- **Professional tree pruning** — including power line clearance pruning
- **Large-scale grass cutting** — for rail, road, and power corridors
- **Herbicide application** — controlled weed and invasive species management

We serve the energy, transport, and municipal sectors with environmentally responsible vegetation management.

---

**Q: Have you worked on Eskom or Gautrain projects?**

**A:** Yes. We have delivered:
- **Eskom vegetation management** — power line servitude clearing and maintenance (via Masteck General Trading)
- **Gautrain grass cutting** — rail corridor maintenance in Soshanguve (via Rospar Trading)

These projects required strict adherence to safety protocols and environmental compliance standards.

---

**Q: Do you handle invasive species management?**

**A:** Yes. We provide invasive alien plant control as part of our integrated vegetation management services. This includes identification, removal, and herbicide treatment of invasive species in compliance with the National Environmental Management: Biodiversity Act (NEMBA).
