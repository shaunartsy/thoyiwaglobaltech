## SUPPORT PROCESS (Section)

### Section Label
Our Process

### Headline (H2)
How We Handle Your Support Request

### Intro Copy
We take every support request seriously. Here's what happens after you submit:

---

### Process Steps (Timeline / Stepper)

**Step 1 — Submission Received**
🔵 Your request is logged in our support system and assigned a unique reference number. You'll receive an email confirmation immediately.

**Step 2 — Assessment & Assignment**
🔵 Our team reviews your request, categorises the priority, and assigns it to the appropriate specialist — whether that's a project manager, technical lead, or accounts administrator.

**Step 3 — Investigation & Response**
🔵 The assigned team member investigates your issue and contacts you within 24 business hours (or immediately for critical/safety issues). We may request additional information or schedule a site visit if needed.

**Step 4 — Resolution & Follow-Up**
🔵 Once resolved, we'll confirm the resolution with you and close the request. We follow up to ensure you're satisfied with the outcome.

---

### Response Time Commitments

| Priority | Initial Response | Target Resolution |
|----------|-----------------|-------------------|
| 🔴 **Critical** | Within 2 hours | Within 24 hours |
| 🟠 **High** | Within 4 hours | Within 48 hours |
| 🟡 **Medium** | Within 24 hours | Within 5 business days |
| 🟢 **Low** | Within 48 hours | Within 10 business days |

*Response times are measured during business hours: Monday – Friday, 07:30 – 17:00.*

### Section Label
Emergency

### Headline (H2)
⚠️ Emergency & After-Hours Support

### Body Copy
For safety-critical emergencies, active site hazards, or situations requiring immediate attention, contact us immediately:

**Emergency Mobile Line:**
📞 **062 430 4206** — Available 24/7 for genuine emergencies

**Emergency Email:**
✉️ **info@thoyiwaglobaltech.co.za** — Subject line: "URGENT: [Brief Description]"

**What Constitutes an Emergency:**
- Active safety hazard on a project site
- Infrastructure failure or damage (e.g., cable cut, structural issue)
- Environmental incident (e.g., chemical spill, uncontrolled vegetation fire)
- Security breach at a project site

**Non-Emergency Matters:**
For routine enquiries, billing questions, and non-urgent issues, please use the support form above or contact us during business hours. This ensures emergency lines remain available for genuine emergencies.
