## SERVICE 2: TELECOMMUNICATIONS INFRASTRUCTURE (Section)

### Section Label
Telecommunications

### Headline (H2)
Connecting South Africa — One Fibre at a Time

### Intro Copy
South Africa's digital future depends on robust, reliable telecommunications infrastructure. Thoyiwa Global Technology's Telecommunications Division is at the forefront of this transformation — delivering fibre, cable, and network infrastructure for the country's leading operators and service providers.

We've installed and maintained networks for **MTN, Vodacom, Telkom, Vumatel**, and **DFA** — working with some of the biggest names in South African telecoms. Our teams are trained, certified, and experienced in the latest installation and maintenance techniques.

---

### Service Detail Cards

---

#### Fibre Floating & Installation

**Headline (H3):** Fibre Floating

**Body:**
We specialise in fibre floating — the precise installation of fibre optic cables through existing duct infrastructure. Our technicians ensure optimal cable placement with minimal signal loss and maximum network performance.

**Applications:**
- FTTH (Fibre to the Home) deployments
- FTTB (Fibre to the Business) installations
- Backbone and distribution network builds
- Network expansion and capacity upgrades

---

#### Cable Jointing

**Headline (H3):** Jointing of Cables

**Body:**
Our certified jointing technicians connect cable segments with precision, ensuring seamless connectivity and long-term reliability. We work with copper, fibre, and hybrid cable systems across underground and aerial networks.

**Applications:**
- Underground and aerial cable joints
- Splice closures and enclosure installations
- Fault repair and cable replacement jointing
- Weatherproofing and environmental protection

---

#### Fibre Splicing

**Headline (H3):** Splicing of Fibres

**Body:**
Fibre splicing is where precision meets performance. Our splicing teams use state-of-the-art fusion splicers to join individual fibre strands with industry-leading accuracy — ensuring low-loss connections that meet or exceed operator specifications.

**Applications:**
- Fusion splicing for single-mode and multi-mode fibres
- Ribbon fibre splicing for high-density applications
- Splice testing and OTDR verification
- Splice closure installation and documentation

---

#### Cable Installation (Full Scope)

**Headline (H3):** End-to-End Cable Installation

**Body:**
From telephone lines to fibre optic backbone, we install the full range of telecommunications cables. Our installation capabilities cover underground, duct, and aerial deployments across residential, commercial, and infrastructure networks.

**Cable Types We Install:**
- Telephone cables
- Coaxial (coax) cables
- Twin flex cables
- Fibre optic cables
- Hybrid fibre-coax (HFC) systems

---

#### Trunking & Conduit Systems

**Headline (H3):** Trunking & PVC Conduit Installation

**Body:**
We install trunking and PVC conduit systems that protect and organise cable infrastructure in commercial buildings, data centres, industrial facilities, and outdoor networks. Our installations meet South African National Standards (SANS) and client specifications.

**Applications:**
- Indoor cable management for commercial and industrial buildings
- Outdoor duct and conduit systems for telecoms networks
- Cable tray and ladder rack installations
- Expansion and retrofit of existing conduit systems

## SERVICE 3: VEGETATION MANAGEMENT (Section)

### Section Label
Vegetation Management

### Headline (H2)
Protecting Infrastructure. Preserving Environments.

### Intro Copy
Vegetation encroachment is one of the leading causes of infrastructure damage, power outages, and service disruptions across South Africa. Thoyiwa Global Technology's Vegetation Management Division provides professional, environmentally responsible clearing, pruning, and maintenance services for utility corridors, transport routes, and public spaces.

We've managed vegetation for **Eskom** power line corridors and **Gautrain** rail infrastructure — demonstrating our ability to operate in high-security, high-compliance environments.

---

### Service Detail Cards

---

#### Tree Pruning

**Headline (H3):** Professional Tree Pruning

**Body:**
Our arboricultural teams provide expert tree pruning services to maintain safe clearances around power lines, buildings, roadways, and rail corridors. We follow industry best practices and environmental regulations to ensure healthy, well-maintained trees that don't compromise infrastructure safety.

**Applications:**
- Power line clearance pruning (Eskom, municipal)
- Road and rail corridor maintenance
- Commercial and residential property management

**Applications:**
