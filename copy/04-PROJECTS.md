**Category Tag:** Telecommunications
**Client:** Ruantel Telecoms (for MTN/Huawei)
**Location:** Sandton · Nelspruit · Krugersdorp · Burgersfort
**Status:** ✅ Completed

**Project Summary:**
One of our flagship telecommunications projects — a large-scale fibre optic installation and ongoing maintenance programme spanning four major regions across Gauteng, Mpumalanga, and Limpopo provinces. **Category Tag:** Telecommunications
**Client:** Matlight Agency (for Vodacom)
**Location:** Heidelberg, Gauteng
**Status:** ✅ Completed

**Key Achievements:**

- Compliant with Vodacom quality standards
