Who We Are

A New Generation of Infrastructure Excellence

Founded in Krugersdorp, Gauteng, Thoyiwa Global Technology (Pty) Ltd is a proudly 100% youth- and female-owned multi-disciplinary service provider. Since 2019, we have been reshaping the infrastructure landscape across construction, telecommunications, and vegetation management.

Our name, Thoyiwa, speaks to our mission: carry forward. We carry forward the ambitions of our clients, the growth of our communities, and the economic empowerment of South Africa's youth and women.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about our services, process, or qualifications? Here are some quick answers.

What services does Thoyiwa Global Technology provide?

We specialise in three core service areas: Construction & Civil Works (trenching, excavation, concrete, formwork), Telecommunications Infrastructure (fibre floating, cable jointing, splicing, FTTH/FTTB installation), and Vegetation Management (tree pruning, grass cutting, herbicide application).

Which network operators have you worked with?

We have delivered projects for South Africa's leading operators including MTN (via Ruantel Telecoms), Vodacom (via Matlight Agency), Telkom/Openserve/Nokia (via Motata and ZealPoint), Vumatel (via TSI), and Dark Fibre Africa (via MCT).

Is Thoyiwa a B-BBEE compliant company?

Yes. Thoyiwa Global Technology is a 100% youth- and female-owned company, qualifying as a Level 1 B-BBEE contributor. We are CIDB registered (10230376), CSD verified (MAAA0966253), and fully SARS tax-compliant.
